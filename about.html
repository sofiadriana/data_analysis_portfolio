About

Hi! I'm Sofia Escalante

Raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, I relocated to Southern California for my undergraduate studies, where I dedicated my academic pursuits to Public Health. Initially aspiring to become a physician, my trajectory shifted upon completion of my degree, as I immersed myself in the roles of a medical scribe and a volunteer in public health research. Through these experiences, I discovered my genuine passion lay in data analysis rather than clinical practice.

Subsequently, I began the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate, during which I developed a profound appreciation for the intricacies of the data analysis process. Being a lifelong learner, I am committed to advancing my skills & proficiency in programming languages, driven by my ambition to evolve into a data scientist.
